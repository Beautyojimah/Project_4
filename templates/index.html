Predictive Analysis for Future Product Sales: A Case of Rossmann

Background

Rossmann is one of Europe's largest drugstore chain operating over 3,000 drug stores across Europe.

Rossmann store managers are tasked with predicting their daily sales for up to six weeks in advance. Store sales are influenced by many factors, including promotions, competition, school and state holidays, seasonality, and locality.

Aim

To develop a predictive model to forecast future sales for up to six weeks in advance based on several factors

Objectives

  • Develop a machine learning model for sales forecasting.
  • Analyse the influence of various factors on sales.
  • Create a user-friendly web application for visualization.
  • Integrate with a database system for real-time analysis.

Business Value

  • optimized operations.
  • cost reduction.
  • informed strategic planning.
